Learn from Creative Professionals

All courses and programs at FACE are led by creative professionals with significant experience in their fields of expertise and top-notch credentials.

Nina Smith

Nina Smith has been the Executive Director of Fine Arts Community Education since 2016. She grew up in our community and learned an appreciation for the arts when she participated in FACE courses as a child.

Nina earned a M.Ed. in Elementary Education at North Carolina State University. She is the instructor for several courses designed for children and teens. Currently, she teaches "Have Fun with Art" and "Maker Culture for Kids."

Robert Jones

Robert Jones is a widely respected art historian who has published scholarly articles on a variety of topics. Currently, his research in architectural studies is focused on the relationship between trades workers rights and building construction technologies.

Dr. Jones earned his doctorate from the School of Architecture at Washington University in St. Louis. Since 2010, he has been the curator of the Sunday Speaker Series at FACE.

Martin Garcia

Martin Garcia was ranked as one of the top 100 artists to watch by Art News magazine. His work has sold at auction for amounts in the tens of thousands. Notably, his paintings "Two Pink Circles on a Yellow Background" and "Lion with Zebra Stripes" are prominently displayed in the lobby of the GloboChem Corporation's headquarters.

Martin earned an M.F.A. from Yale University. He has been the primary instructor for "Learning Painting" and "Learning Photography" since 2013.

Michelle Davis

Michelle Davis is the first chair trumpeter in the City Symphony. She earned a B.A. in Music Performance from the University of Wisconsin at Madison. She has been the director of the FACE orchestra since 2019.

Kevin Thompson

Kevin Thompson is a popular stand-up comic and sketch comedian. He studied at the Second City in Chicago.

Before taking an interest in comedy, Kevin had success in musical theater. He was a member of several casts on and off Broadway. You might recognize him from his role in Cats.

Kevin teaches "Comedic Improv" and is the director the Performing Arts department.
